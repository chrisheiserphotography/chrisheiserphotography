About Me

I'm originally from Phoenix, AZ. I graduated from NAU with a BS in Computer Science. I've lived between Flagstaff, Prescott, and Bend Oregon ever since. My first camera was a disposable film camera, and though I dropped photography a few times when I was younger it always drew me back.


Landscape photos were my main focus at the beginning, but I've since branched out to many other types of photos. I love fly fishing photography; Every fish, fish pattern, fight, and situation is unique. Photos that are one-offs with a time-sensitive aspect are my favorites.
