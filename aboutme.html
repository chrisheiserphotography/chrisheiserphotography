About me

Code, photography, and fly fishing

That's the short version. The longer version is that I spent some years getting a BS in Computer Science at NAU and living in Flagstaff, AZ. I picked a camera back up a few years ago after a long hiatus from disposable and early digital cameras. I picked a fly rod up a couple years ago after an even longer hiatus from spin fishing as a kid. I fortunately moved to Bend, Oregon and was surrounded in fish and beautiful landscapes.

I've come to realize these things are more than hobbies to me, and I'll be doing them for the rest of my life. I've learned to embrace them and continually better myself. Enjoy!
